Prototype Update

Prototype Validation

The prototype that we're fabricating will validate that using airfoil sections in cloth is enough to provide a strong wing structure that can carry aerodynamic forces while being flexible enough to be altered for control purposes. The prototype is a scaled down model of a full wind turbine blade, which we will use to demonstrate the twisting capability. We intend to show how the use of spars and a few rigid airfoil shaped sections can maintain the blade shape and move in a desired fashion. We have completed a bill of materials, and soon the components necessary for fabrication will be ordered. Our design is fairly feasable to build, and photos from the fabrication process will be posted here as we move forward on that process. The finished prototype will also be posted here to demonstrate the movement we've discussed.
