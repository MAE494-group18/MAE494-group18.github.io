Specifications

Performance Requirement Description
Blade Twist Performance The turbine blade will have 6 degrees of twist for every fifth of blade length.
Energy Output Performance 3285 MWh of energy generated from turbine operations.
Operational Life A theoretical lifespan of ten years is desired.
Noise Noise generated by the turbine must not exceed 45 dB.
Demonstration Resources Electrical power is necessary to demonstrate the twisting blade. A current of 15 A with 110 V at 60 Hz is desired.

Description of Specifications

The purpose of the active twist turbine blade system is to improve the efficiency of turbine blades by providing an axis of rotation of about six degrees about the blade spar. The turbine blade we are fabricating will optimize the angle of attack at which the blade catches the wind and alter this angle to stop the motion of the turbine in violent or high wind conditions.

For prototype fabrication, we intend to use a mix of commercially available components and our own fabricated parts. Commercially available components are useful because they are standardized and specifications are given, as well as drawings to fit into our assembly drawing. We are also able to avoid added complexity. Components that we fabricate as a group will involve the process of 3D printing, milling, sawing, drilling, grinding, stitching, and adhesive bonding. These techniques will be utilized as they are effective for the level of complexity of our fabricated components.
