Scheduling and Task Management

Our group has been mostly sticking to our original planned schedule that we starteed the semester with. We purposefully set our planned finishing dates long before the actual due date, as to allow for review and revisions within the group. As the project progressed, less time is needed for review from the group. For deadlines that we miss we are able to quickly delegate tasks based on who is the most currently available and finish the task promptly. We continue to remain in constant communication and verify with one another that any outstanding tasks are completed and that we are allotting ourselves reasonable time to fulfill our responsibilities in the project. So far our missed deadlines have not hindered our ability to efficiently complete tasks.
